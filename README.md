# Ego

### About
Ego is a 2D platform game that showcases the beginning of our protagonist, Nim's, adventure. He was a Raccoon that had a normal life, until something extremely strange happened. He lost his form and was transformed into a disturbing lifeless shape. However, our character noticed that something was off, and after finding a raccoon's mask, he regained his original form. After that, he had a mission, finding whoever did this and making him pay for it.

### Controls
A and D - To walk
Space - To jump
Enter - To interact
1 and 2 - To use masks
Esc - To pause
